Surgery Types

Total Knee Replacement

Replaces damaged joint surfaces with artificial parts.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive using a camera and small tools.

Key Benefits

Improved Movement

Better joint function after surgery.

Reduced Pain

Can help lower chronic joint pain.

Q&A

How long is the recovery?

Recovery can take weeks to months depending on your condition.

Are there any risks?

Yes. Risks include infection, bleeding, or clotting.
