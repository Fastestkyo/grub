Surgery Types

Total Knee Replacement

Involves replacing damaged joint surfaces with prosthetics.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive method using a scope and small tools.

Key Benefits

Improved Movement

Patients often regain better joint function post-surgery.

Reduced Pain

Surgery can significantly lower chronic joint pain.

Q&A

How long is the recovery?

Recovery can take weeks to months depending on your condition.

Are there any risks?

Like any surgery, risks include infection, bleeding, or clotting.
